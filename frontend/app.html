What can I do for you today?

Select all the tasks that apply to your dataset. You can combine them.

Preparing data usually takes the largest share of any analysis — often more than half the work — and most of that time is spent checking and tidying. DataSousChef helps with that step in two ways: it describes what your data is like and finds quality problems for you to fix.

You describe your data by answering short questions. Nothing about your data ever leaves your computer. You then get a Python script to run yourself.

Option 1 — Diagnose & Standardise: A step-by-step guide to find hidden problems in your dataset. For example, extra spaces in column headings, or values that mean the same thing but are written differently — like F, f, Female and female in a Gender column.

Option 2 — Cross-Column Checks: For more complex datasets with columns that relate to each other. For example, comparing an Age column and a Date of Birth column to check they agree, or spotting unusual values.

Option 3 — Link Datasets: Helps you decide how to join two or more files — which column to use as the link, and whether either file has duplicates that would cause problems.
